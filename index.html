Mac and Cheese

Mac And Cheese is as delicious as it as simple!

ingredients:

  1. Cheese sauce
  2. Macaroni Noodles
  3. Boiling water
  4. Pot

Steps:

  1. Add water to pot.
  2. Boil water until bubbling
  3. Add macaroni and boil for 3-4 minutes
  4. Pour out water
  5. Add cheese sauce

I know what you're thinking; that's too easy to be true! Well, you better believe it. That is how you make Mac And Cheese.
