Nachos

So easy to make, even better to eat!

Ingredients:

  1. Cheese
  2. Chips
  3. Butter
  4. Milk
  5. Pot

Steps:

  1. Add butter to heated pot
  2. Add cheese to pot
  3. Add milk to pot
  4. Stir cheese and milk until smooth consistency
  5. Arrange chips on a bowl or plate
  6. Pour cheese dip on top of chips
